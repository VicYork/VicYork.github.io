Who is this guy?

This is what I do!

Victor is a Software Engineer at Culturalyst specializing in full-stack development. My experience include working with technologies such Angular, React, Backbone on the front-end, as well as Node/Express, Mysql, MongoDB, Go on the backend.

Here's a little more

As a native New Orleanian, AmeriCorps Alum, having served with Propeller Incubator, he has worked in the non-profit sector for over 8 years as a student, activist, and educator all over the United States with a focus on civic engagement, democratic education, and science literacy. He began his work as a founding member of a New Orleans based non-profit youth organization, Kids Rethink New Orleans Schools, tackling education reform post-Hurricane Katrina recovery. Since then, he has worked in collaboration with many organizations both locally, such as Operation Spark, GrowDat, and Young Artist Young Aspirations, as well as national coalitions such as Hack Reactor, the Alliance for Educational Justice, Movement Strategy Centre, and the Funders’ Collaborative on Youth Organizing. He continues his advocacy work on youth leadership as an adult supporter and as the co-leader of the National Coordinating Committee – Youth Sector in response to the White House’s My Brother’s Keeper initiative through the Executive’ Alliance.
